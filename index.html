About me

Hello, My name is Zeljko Provic, I was born and raised in a small country in Serbia in 1996.

Not to lengthen much, my current occupation as in the past 6 years is a sports equipment salesman.

A year and a half ago, I became interested in programming and creating websites, and since then I have been actively working on improving the skills needed for that.

I put a lot of time and effort into my personal development, I learn quickly and interpersonal communication is definitely one of my virtues, I believe that a good atmosphere and communication in the team is the key to successful business.

I am very ambitious and I hope that someone will recognize it and give me the opportunity to prove it.

If you are interested in my story, you can fill out the contact form at the bottom of the page or visit my Linkedin profile where you can find out more details about my current skills.
